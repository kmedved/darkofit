Commit `671f2e0` made `NEXT_STEPS.md` the binding forward decision record. Its
commit-message phrase “planning-only for all execution rows” described the
commit's code-free documentation diff. It did **not** revoke the future work

parameters, exports, public documentation, or a released package surface.
