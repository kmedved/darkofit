# Panel-3 declaration drafts rejected before freeze

## Evidence boundary

The rejected drafts were metadata-only until the later pre-H1 integrity
incident recorded below. Source descriptions, schemas, task and dataset IDs,
declared targets, row counts, and split definitions were reviewed. No target
value was decoded or materialized, no target preflight artifact was created,
and no candidate, control, or comparator was fit or scored. Parquet-footer
target minimum/maximum statistics were inadvertently observed for three
otherwise eligible declarations; their values are not recorded here, and all
three lineages are permanently excluded from the prospective pool.

None of the task orders below has standing. The only prospective order is the
one in `panel3_registry_declarations.json`, after its source and transform code
is committed and its exact target-free split indices are materialized and
hash-bound by the registry.

## Why the first dual-candidate authorization is blocked

The first power calculation treated all 12 selected lineages as exchangeable
draws from the 15-profile T5 development pool. That is not the panel the
candidate will actually see. Four selected tasks—energy efficiency, forest
fires, wheat, and USNEWS colleges—have fewer than 2,000 outer-training rows
on every coordinate and therefore trigger the composite policy's
prospectively fixed size-gate decline. Conditioning the same design simulation
on eight empirical draws plus four exact ties reduces the simulated joint-gate
pass probability from `0.949395` to approximately `0.713`, below the declared
`0.80` floor. After the pre-H1 exclusions below promoted three small reserves,
the recovered panel has seven fixed size-gate ties and five empirical draws;
the same provisional diagnostic is `58893 / 200000 = 0.294465`.

The standalone guarded-cross calculation was also not an authorization-grade
power basis. It sampled only the three smooth datasets on which that mechanism
had already been nominated, so it encoded winner selection as a 100% simulated
pass probability. Even an optimistic safe-decline sensitivity that assumes
the 12 non-nominee development profiles would be exact ties gives only about
`0.473` at 12 lineages. The repository contains no broader exact standalone
guarded-cross outcome pool.

No Panel-3 outcome was inspected to discover either defect. The corrective
step is a source-frozen, development-only census on the already-spent
13-dataset TabArena regression panel, running the exact current control and
both exact candidate policies on three predeclared coordinates. Every
engagement, decline, tie, loss, and size-gated no-op must enter the resulting
effect pool. Panel 3 remains unauthorized until that calibration is complete,
its fixed-panel power calculation is frozen prospectively, and every other
source and contamination gate passes.

## Why the first 20-task draft was rejected

Independent review found defects that were knowable without outcomes:

- `kegg_metabolic_directed` used an arbitrary numbered graph column as the
  target; the cited undirected-network source did not establish that target as
  a scientific prediction outcome.
- SAT11 and ASP-POTASSCO runtime rows repeat algorithm instances, include
  run-status/censoring concerns, and require instance-grouped evaluation rather
  than their uploaded random folds.
- The CERN invariant-mass target is deterministically reconstructible from
  included collision-vector components.
- `wind`, `bank8FM`, and Zurich delays were already present in bound
  DarkoFit/ChimeraBoost evidence catalogs.
- Beijing PM, Bangladesh rainfall, stock inventory, retail sales, and similar
  panels required time/entity splits that the draft did not specify.
- The law-school, review-rating, and several gameplay targets were discrete or
  ordinal labels uploaded under regression, not the intended continuous
  regression outcomes.
- SUPPORT and related survival-style tasks exposed censoring/status semantics
  that were not handled by scalar RMSE regression.
- Several applied tasks had weak or unverifiable provenance. In particular,
  `NewFuelCar` was described only as “Testing this plattform.”

Those are design failures, not bad model results. Reserves cannot be used to
paper over defects already visible before freeze, so the complete order was
discarded.

## Why the proposed seven-task CTR23 reuse became six

The frozen CTR23 contamination registry originally marked seven lockbox tasks
eligible. The current local ChimeraBoost repository is newer than that
decision. ChimeraBoost v0.15.0 explicitly reviewed `socmob` in
`benchmarks/HIGHCARD_PLAN.md` and cut it as a small low-cardinality regression
candidate. Panel 3 treats catalog inspection as exposure even when no model fit
was retained. Therefore task `361264` is excluded and receives no stale
lockbox exception.

The six CTR23 tasks retained by the replacement declaration remain sealed with
respect to DarkoFit candidate outcomes:

- Naval propulsion, wave energy, SARCOS, and energy efficiency;
- CPS88 wages and forest fires.

Their inclusion is narrow, not a claim that all are real-world deployment
tests. Naval, wave, and energy are simulator datasets. SARCOS official random
folds measure dense trajectory-point interpolation rather than cold-trajectory
generalization. Those limitations are binding semantic caveats in the
declarations and must appear in any result.

## Why three otherwise eligible declarations were excluded before H1

Structural review inadvertently exposed Parquet-footer target minimum/maximum
statistics for Mercari task `363377`, Google QA task `363370`, and Sberbank
task `363495`. No statistic value is retained in the declaration or protocol,
but the exposure violates the prospective target-statistic boundary. All three
tasks, datasets, and lineage clusters are therefore permanently ineligible
for Panel 3 and cannot be used as reserves.

Replacement was mechanical within stratum and fixed before H1: Mercari's
old-priority slot promoted the first untouched mixed reserve, Garment
Productivity task `360993`; Google QA's slot promoted the next untouched mixed
reserve, Sensory task `359931`; and Sberbank's slot promoted the first
untouched applied reserve, Baseball Hitter task `4851`. The exact exclusion
ledger and same-stratum replacement mapping are fail-closed in both the
declaration and validator.

## Source-audited replacement

The prospective declaration is a hybrid panel:

- six still-sealed CTR23 lineages under their exact official folds; and
- six new, source-reviewed lineages: Garment Productivity, Sensory, Imperial
  loan-default loss, UK wheat quality, USNEWS colleges, and Baseball Hitter.

The new tasks use explicit target-free policies:

- Garment Productivity uses expanding chronological date blocks and a frozen
  target-free calendar transform.
- Sensory groups repeated evaluations by wine halfplot and removes the grouping
  key from the fitted feature view.
- Loan-default removes its unique ID and uses a fixed target-free shuffled
  split, consistent with the source statement that observations are
  independent.
- Wheat groups by variety and excludes same-assay or later bread-quality
  outputs.
- The exact OpenML task view for USNEWS colleges already excludes its
  federal/name identifiers upstream, so no local transform is applied. Its
  normalized-name alarm against catalog dataset `colleges` is manually
  adjudicated as a source distinction: USNEWS 1993–94 graduation-rate data
  (dataset `538`) versus U.S. College Scorecard Pell-grant data (datasets
  `42159`/`42727`). The registry must still prove distinct dataset IDs, source
  families, and semantic fingerprints.
- Baseball Hitter uses the exact task feature view, which already excludes the
  unique player-name field, and seeded shuffled folds over one row per player.

The date parser, group keys, group balancing, feature drops, and every final
train/test index are part of the prospective source contract. The runner may
consume only the registry-materialized explicit indices; it may not regenerate
or alter them at execution time.
